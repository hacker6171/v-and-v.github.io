# Articles & Photojournalism Portfolio

This repository hosts a web page showcasing my journalism and photography work during my graduate studies at **Eastern Illinois University**.

From **January 2022 to May 2023**, I worked as a **Journalist Photographer** for **The Daily Eastern News**, covering campus events, student activities, and university milestones through articles and visual storytelling.

This project serves as a digital archive of my published work and highlights my experience in media, communication, and documentation alongside my background in technology.
