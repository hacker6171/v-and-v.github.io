About My Work

From January 2022 to May 2023, while pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Technology at Eastern Illinois University, I worked as a Journalist Photographer for The Daily Eastern News.

During this time, I covered campus events, student activities, and community stories through photography and written articles. This experience strengthened my storytelling, communication, and observation skills while balancing academic responsibilities.

My work focused on documenting real moments, capturing university life, and contributing to the student media community.
